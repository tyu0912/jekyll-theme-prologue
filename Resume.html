Hi. I’m Tennison and I love working with data. I’m currently a data analyst by day and a Masters of Information and Data Science (MIDS) student by night. I first got interested in data while running experiments as a researcher.

In research, a big part of the job is to discern insight from the experiments you run and I’ve always found that part to be very interesting. After several years of research, I decided to move fully into an analytical role and haven’t looked back since.

Now, I use data to help companies find insight whether it be through ad-hoc reporting or long-term data modeling. When I’m not crunching numbers, I also like to read, cook, and go on hikes.
